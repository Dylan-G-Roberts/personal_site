Hello there!

Dylan Roberts is currently working as a fullstack developer at Guild Software Inc. He is passionate about a wide array of topics, especially software engineering, history, geopolitics, sci-fi, skiing, bushcraft, and / hiking.

He was born and raised in Farmington Maine, during which he developed a love of sports, computers, and the outdoors. After graduating high school he studied Computer Science at <a href="https://www.dal.ca">Dalhousie University</a> in Canada.

At the start of the SARS-CoV-2 global pandemic, he started work as a software developer at Guild. After graduating in September 2020, he focused his efforts on further developing his software engineering and computer science skills.

He currently is auditing MIT courses: <a href="https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/6.824/">6.824 Distributed Systems</a>, and <a href="https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/electrical-engineering-and-computer-science/6-006-introduction-to-algorithms-fall-2011/">6.006 Introduction to Algorithms</a>, he is working with Typescript and React to build a browser-based Javascript coding environment.
